About Me

Hi, my name is Phillip Ngo and I'm a sophomore studying Computer Science at Virginia Tech.
I created this site as a result of spending hours freshman year trying to make a schedule with no Friday classes and I hope you find it useful too.

Along with redesigning the look of the site, I plan on adding a ton of new capabilities to it. Here are a few:

  • Minimum gap time between classes
  • The ability to choose a specific time and day NOT to have class
  • A new and improved Timetable for general course searching
  • Hand making schedules by adding courses from the new timetable


If you have any problems with the website, have any ideas I could implement, want to collab, or just check out the code, send me an email or check out the project on github below. Thanks!
